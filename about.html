Our Story

We the People Stage

We the People Stage (WTPS) is a registered non-profit trust in India committed to promoting education, health, and socio-economic development, with a special focus on the Public Policy. We work to create awareness of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and conduct vocational and skill training programs for underprivileged communities and students—irrespective of caste, creed, colour, or religion.

Our mission is to build capabilities, enhance livelihoods, and empower individuals to contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Purpose

WTPS also partners with organizations, NGOs, and social enterprises to design customized advisory and learning solutions that strengthen human capital and organizational effectiveness. Through our capacity-building initiatives, we help NGOs adapt to global challenges with scalable, sustainable, and impact-driven programs that foster inclusive growth and nation-building.

Mission

To promote education, empowerment, and ethical governance through research, training, and community-driven programs that strengthen democratic values.

Vision

To create a vibrant, cultural, religious, Social Values informed, and empowered society where every citizen contributes to nation-building and sustainable development.

Our Inspiration

Guided by Vision & Service

Shri Keshav Vinayagan Ji

A Lifetime Dedicated to Service and Nation-Building

Shri Keshava Vinayagan Ji stands as the guiding force behind the formation of this socially oriented reforming trust. Inspired by his vision, the organisation was established to serve society through a structured framework that promotes research-driven policies for a welfare state.

With over four and a half decades of dedicated service to the nation, Shri Keshava Vinayagan Ji began his journey in 1980 as an RSS Pracharak and went on to serve in key roles such as Jilla, Vibhag, and Prantha Pracharak. His life has been a continuous pursuit of organisational growth, national reconstruction, and social upliftment.

Since 2002, he has been serving as the State Organising Secretary of Seva Bharathi Tamil Nadu, where he led numerous humanitarian projects. His leadership was instrumental in rehabilitating tsunami-affected families by constructing and handing over 834 homes, reflecting his compassion and administrative excellence.

Committed to the welfare of the poor and the empowerment of youth, he has guided many students toward higher education and social responsibility. Since 2016, as the State Organising General Secretary of the BJP Tamil Nadu, he continues to embody the principles of discipline, selfless service, and nation-building—strengthening the vision of a self-reliant and prosperous Tamil Nadu.

Leadership

Meet Our Managing Trustee

Mr. P. Senthil Kumar

Managing Trustee

With over two decades of experience in public policy and social development, Dr. Kumar envisions a society where governance is transparent, inclusive, and responsive to the needs of every citizen. His leadership has been instrumental in bridging the gap between policy-makers and communities, ensuring that grassroots voices shape the decisions that affect their lives.

Under his guidance, We the People Stage has grown into a powerful platform for democratic engagement, empowering thousands of citizens to actively participate in shaping India's future. His commitment to sustainable development and social justice continues to inspire our work across education, health, and electoral reforms.
